Kimchi. This Will blow your mind.

Kimchi (김치 - Hangul) also spelled kimchee or gimchi, is a traditional Korean dish. It is used as a side dish made of vegetables with a variety of seasonings. In traditional preparations, kimchi was stored underground in jars to keep cool during the summer months and unfrozen during the winter months. There are hundreds of varieties of kimchi made from napa cabbage, radish, scallion, or cucumber as the main ingredients.
